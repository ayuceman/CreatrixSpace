### 1. **Google Hasn't Indexed Your Site Yet** ⚠️ MOST LIKELY ISSUE

**Problem:** Even with all SEO fixes, Google needs to:
- Discover your site
- Crawl it
- Index it
- Rank it

**This takes TIME** - typically 1-4 weeks after first crawl, sometimes longer.

**Immediate Actions:**

#### A. Verify Google Search Console Setup
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Check if property `https://creatrixventures.space` is added
3. If not added:
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Enter: `https://creatrixventures.space`
   - Choose verification method (HTML tag is already in your site)
   - Click "Verify"

#### B. Submit Sitemap
1. In Google Search Console, go to "Sitemaps" (left sidebar)
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click "Submit"
4. Wait for status to show "Success"

#### C. Request Indexing for Key Pages
1. Go to "URL Inspection" tool
2. Enter these URLs one by one and click "Request Indexing":
   - `https://creatrixventures.space/`
   - `https://creatrixventures.space/locations`
   - `https://creatrixventures.space/locations/dhobighat-hub` (if exists)

#### D. Check Coverage Report
1. Go to "Coverage" in left sidebar
2. Check if pages are indexed
3. Fix any errors shown
